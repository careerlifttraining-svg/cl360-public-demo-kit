# CL360 Workflow Examples

## Workflow 1: Podcast Episode → Social Campaign

1. Record episode
2. Export audio/video
3. Pull best quotes
4. Create short clips
5. Write captions
6. Design promo graphics
7. Schedule posts
8. Ask audience discussion question

---

## Workflow 2: Artist Interview Campaign

1. Confirm guest
2. Collect approved image and social handles
3. Create promo graphic
4. Publish teaser
5. Record interview
6. Pull quote cards
7. Release episode
8. Publish follow-up content

---

## Workflow 3: Music Review Content

1. Select artist/song/project
2. Research background
3. Record review
4. Pull strongest opinion
5. Create short-form clip
6. Ask audience if they agree
